IMS Advocacy: A seat at the table

Every IMS™ client is entitled to an IMS Advocate. Available at no cost to you.

Request an IMS advocate

Who are they?

IMS Advocates are folks that work at the IMS lab.

IMS Developers

IMS Testers

IMS Level 2 support

IMS Product managers

What do they do?

An IMS Advocates job is to help IMS clients.

1

They are there to get you what you need to be successful with IMS.

2

A good IMS advocate is a lobbyist for their IMS clients.

3

IMS advocates ensure to have regular calls with their clients.

4

IMS advocates are NOT salespeople, they don't earn commission.

Examples of advocacy

A client made their Db2 DBA in charge of IMS.


Advocacy action

The IMS team set up educational webinars for Db2 DBA's.

A client requested to have a DEDB greater than 2K AREAs.

Advocacy action

Enhancement released to increase DEDBs storage to 9,999 AREAs.

A client opened a case for an issue regarding a PoC.

Advocacy action

Made sure to escalate the matter to get attention to the case and to get it resolved sooner.
