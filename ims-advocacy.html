Who are they?

IMS Advocates are folks that work at the IMS lab.

IMS developers

IMS testers

IMS support

IMS product managers

What do they do?

An IMS advocates job is to help IMS clients.

They can help you be more successful with IMS.

They lobby for their clients.

There is no cost to the client.

They are not salespeople; they don't earn commissions.

Examples of advocacy

Challenge

A Db2 DBA needed to learn how to run IMS databases.

Advocacy action

Advocate assembled live and self-paced IMS database education for the client.

Challenge

Client requested that their DEDBs have more than 2,048 areas per database.

Advocacy action

Advocate pushed for an enhancement to allow DEDBs to have 9,999 areas per database.

Challenge

Client needed a quick turnaround for a proof of concept.

Advocacy action

Advocate helped escalate the requirement to resolve the situation sooner.
