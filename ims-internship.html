About the IMS Internship

The IMS customer internship at SVL is an opportunity for customers to improve on, grow, and solidify their IMS skills and expertise. Our goal is to enhance our customer’s understanding of new and existing functions, features, and capabilities in ways that are best suited for their shop. In 2020, we aim to enhance the hands-on experience and value offered from last year’s internship. You can view what was covered in the 2019 internship here.
