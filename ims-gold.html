IMS Gold

A client feedback program shaping the future of IMS™.

Enroll

How do you benefit from IMS Gold?

As an IMS Gold feedback client, you can help shape the features and enhancements most relevant to your infrastructure and business needs, for example:

  • As a sponsor user, you collaborate with the IMS development team throughout the development cycle, not just in the early design phases.
  • As an IMS Gold feedback client, you get updates on what's new in the IMS development pipeline.

How does it work?

You'll meet with the IMS team regularly. Members also get access to the IMS Gold community on Slack and discussion forums. And the best part is that there's no fee to participate in the IMS Gold program.

In the Gold meetings, you will get:

  • Status on new and in-progress IMS enhancements
  • Information on new initiatives and solutions
  • Opportunities to provide the IMS team with feedback

Enroll now

Before you enroll, understand the requirements:

  • Be sure you have support from your organization to attend regular meetings (1-2 hours per month) and potentially act as a sponsor user.
  • You must be an application developer, system programmer, enterprise architect, database administrator, or a business executive.

Here is how to enroll.

Be sure you have an IBM ID.

Sign up for the IBM Z® Client Feedback Program (zCF).

Decide which IBM Z products, including IMS, you want to provide feedback for. Note that IMS is listed as part of Operating systems and application software.

Submit the IMS Gold enrollment form.

Questions? Contact Joe Peacock: joseph.peacock@ibm.com

That's it — let the collaboration begin!
